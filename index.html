Overview

The 3rd Workshop on Control for Networked Computing Systems (CNC) aims to bring together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of control theory, computer systems, and networking.

Topics of Interest

  • Control-based resource management in cloud computing
  • Feedback control in computer networks
  • Learning and control for distributed systems
  • Autonomous and self-adaptive systems
  • Control for edge and real-time systems

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: August 15, 2025
  • Notification: September 20, 2025
  • Camera-ready: October 10, 2025
  • Workshop Date: December 8, 2025

Submission Guidelines

Submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere. Papers should follow the ACM CoNEXT formatting and be up to 6 pages including references. Submit via HotCRP at: https://cnc2025.hotcrp.com
